About West Loch Park Vigan

As you enter into the luxurious West Loch Park Hotel after a long day of roaming around the City of Vigan, we provide the comfort and relaxation you wish to experience. The simplicity of its architecture makes you, our guests, feel the cozy vibe and elegance that the company wants to showcase. West Loch Park Hotel Vigan has 15 comfortable with a touch of sophistication guest rooms that are secured by a high-tech key card security system to keep you, safe, secured and relaxed at all times. We also offer a variety of Western and Asian cuisine that will fit your tastes and preference that the other hotels in town doesn't have. Rooms: Standard Room : 22 square meters one full bed, bathroom with bidet, flat screen TV, air conditioning, WIFI, a safe room guarded with high technology keycard system. Superior Room: 27 square meters one queen bed, bathroom with bidet, veranda with a view of the streets of Vigan, spacious, sofa chair, flat screen TV, air conditioning, WIFI, a safe room guarded with high technology keycard system. Deluxe Room: 31 square meters two twin beds, bathroom with bidet, flat screen TV, air conditioning, WIFI, a safe room guarded with high technology keycard system.
